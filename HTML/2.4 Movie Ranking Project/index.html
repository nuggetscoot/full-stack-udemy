Poppy Yuri Review

Otherside Picnic

I did like the plot, but there was barely any yuri. I guess it was implied at the end, but I thought it would've been more prevalent based on the cover art. I heard they are canon in the manga though which raises the score a little bit.

Score: 8/10

I'm In Love With The Villainess

I can understand why people like it. I thought it was cute and pretty funny. I really don't understand why they chose to add incest, it literally added nothing to the plot. That's the only thing that threw me off about it, it was good besides that. I did like the premise of there being a bunch of boys for her to choose from, but she chose the girl that bullies her.

Score: 7.5/10

Sweet Blue Flowers

The art style is super cute. It's the first one on the list with an actual kiss, but it's not between the two main girls. It was definitely a cute story, but as someone who prefers watching shows with action, I found it a little boring. If you're someone who just wants a cute story of girls coming to terms with their sexuality, you'll probably like this.

Score: 8/10

Stardust Telepath

So incredibly cute. Akeuchi is super autistic coded, which I really appreciated. While they are never confirmed to be canon, they almost kiss multiple times and it's very heavily implied that Umika has a crush on Akeuchi. Also surprisingly funny, it made me laugh out loud a few times. The ending did feel a little rushed, but I enjoyed it overall.

Score: 9/10

Adachi And Shimamura

I feel like this one is a classic. I have the same problem as I had with Sweet Blue Flowers, where I just found it a little boring because there was so action. But it was by no means bad, I still enjoyed watching it for the most part. I don't understand why people call this yuri bait. They don't really get together at the end, but it's made explicitly clear that Adachi is in love with Shimamura. I agree with most people that the ending was unsatisfying, but they get together in the light novel which makes it better.

Score: 8/10

The Many Sides Of Voice Actor Radio

Speaking of yuri bait, this one fits the bill for me. It had so much potential in the beginning. I loved their dynamic of rivals who have to pretend to like each other, I just wish they went through with it at the end and actually made them canon. There is a little bit of subtext, but they basically admitted at the end that it was all bait. Also the fanservice at the beginning made me very uncomfortable because they're high schoolers, but it calmed down about halfway through.

Score: 6/10

Konohana Kitan

Surprisingly dark, pretty good but could use more yuri

The Magical Revolution Of The Reincarnated Princess And The Genius

So good had angst, humor, action, and an actual kiss

Izetta The Last Witch

Good action and soundtrack but the fanservice was out of control

Inugami-San to Nekoyama-San

Pretty forgettable but cute I guess

Birdie Wing

As a golf hater, this was actually way better than expected, could have used a little more yuri but it's pretty heavily implied

If My Favorite Pop Idol Made It To The Budokan, I Would Die

Super funny and relatable, yuri super prevalent but the ending left a lot unresolved

Vampire In The Garden

Literally the definition of doomed yuri I almost cried

Mobile Suit Gundam: The Witch From Mercury

I guess they're technically canon but it barely focused on their relationship so I'm a little hesitant to call it yuri, the plot was good though

BanG Dream It's My Go!!!

Idk why but this was one of my favorites, I love autistic lesbians

Lycoris Recoil

Good action and some yuri subtext, it just didn't stand out to me

Asteroid In Love

Literally the defintion of yuri fluff, just so cute and also Ao trans and Suzu is so me

Liz And The Blue Bird

I could see it being misconstrued as a friendship, but I thought it was a super cute unrequited love story

Mysteria Friends

Really dissapointed bc I loved their dynamic so much, but there was too much fanservice and barely a plot. Would love to see an actual full-fledged anime with them

Strawberry Panic

Sometimes written like a bad fanfiction but I think that's part of the charm of it
